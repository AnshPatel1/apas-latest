Faculty Appraisal Cycle Timeline

Phase 1

Verification Stage

This stage is to verify only the prepopulated parameters and get it rectified in case of any discrepancies

Self Appraisal

This stage is to fill in the remaining parameters other than the prepopulated parameters as a part of your self appraisal

Submission

All parameters are submitted to the respecting Reporting Officers.

Phase 2

Reporting Officer Evaluation

This stage is for the Reporting Officers to evaluate your self appraisal submission.

Submit to Reviewing Officer

Your self-appraisal is verified by your Reporting Officer and is now sent to be reviewed by your Reviewing Officer.

Phase 3

Reviewing Officer Evaluation

This stage is for the Reviewing Officers to evaluate your self appraisal submission alongwith the Reporting Officer's Evaluation

Final Submission

The evaluation by the Reviewing Officer shall be considered full and final.

Phase 4

Result Declaration

The results are published at this stage and are available at status section on the sidebar for your kind reference.
